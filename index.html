Origami Camel

Fun facts:

  1. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  2. Camels can completely shut their nostrils during sandstorms.
  3. Camels store fat, not water in their humps

Origami Chameleon

Fun facts:

  1. They mainly change color in order to communicate or regulate body temperature.
  2. Their feet are shaped like tongs.
  3. Their eyes can swivel around in two different directions simultaneously.
